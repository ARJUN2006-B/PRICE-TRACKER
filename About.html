About Price Tracker

Price Tracker is your one-stop platform for finding the best product deals online. Our tool helps you monitor price changes across major e-commerce platforms like Amazon, Flipkart, Meesho, and more — saving your time and money.

We are dedicated to providing users with accurate, real-time data and a smooth experience to make online shopping smarter and simpler.

💡 Mission

To help users make smarter purchasing decisions through transparent price tracking.

🚀 Vision

To become India’s most trusted price comparison and deal alert platform.

🤝 Values

Transparency, simplicity, and commitment to helping online shoppers save more.
